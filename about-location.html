Green Life Farm

A small farm located between Edinburgh and Carlisle has been converted to a visitor attraction that includes a shop selling local produce and plants, small petting zoo, cafe, hotel and walks.

We offer a variety of activities and entertainment. Whether you are visiting us for the first time or you are a regular guest, you can be sure that you will always find something unique here. Rent any of our lodges and enjoy your holiday!
