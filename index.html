Our Facilities

Shop & Cafe

We have a shop and café on our farm, offering the freshest and best local food and drink throughout the day. We are open from morning until late evening during the summer season.

Free Breakfast

We are known for our breakfasts, which we serve each day from 9:30 to 11:00. We respect our guests and can offer a diversified menu with something special to satisfy any whimsical taste.

Lunch & Light Bites

We offer a wide range of starters, snacks, light bites, main courses, and incredibly delicious desserts Monday to Saturday. We use only seasonal ingredients and change our menu daily.
